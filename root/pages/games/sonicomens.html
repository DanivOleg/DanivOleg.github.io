Description

This fan-made game tells the story of why chaos emeralds are no longer used in Sonic series games until 2022 and what events happened between Sonic Unleashed and Sonic Colors.

Eggman is up to his old tricks again and steals the Chaos Emeralds. Sonic and Tails are on their way to stop him! But something is wrong here... Why did he need the emeralds so urgently? Why did G.U.N. send Agent Shadow after him?

And who are these strange monsters that attack everyone who happens to be near the Gaia Temples?
